After a successful debut featuring 100+ participants and 150+ debates, SSDC League of Spars returns with Season 2—more competitive, structured, and impactful.

Organized entirely by students, LOS S2 reflects the strength of youth leadership, collaboration, and intellectual exchange.

The league follows the AP ISC format where every institution competes against every other institution once during the season.

All preliminary rounds will be online via Discord while the Finals will be held physically.

  • Participation in every debate is mandatory.
  • All clubs must review the rules and submit required forms.
  • The league will follow AP ISC format.
  • All preliminary rounds will be held online via Discord.
  • Failure to attend within deadline results in walkover.
  • One week out of every four will be a silent week.
  • A nominal registration fee will be collected.
  • Finals will be held physically.
  • Each institution must maintain at least 5 debaters.
  • Every debater must participate in at least two-thirds of debates.
  • Participation should be reasonably balanced.
  • Executives must coordinate schedules with opponents.
  • Additional activities may include matter sessions and motion discussions.
  • Special recognitions like Debater of the Month will be awarded.
